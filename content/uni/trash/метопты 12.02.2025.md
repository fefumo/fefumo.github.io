Найти наибольшее и наименьшее значения функции $z = xy$ в области $x^2 + y^2 \leq 1$
$$
\begin{cases}
z'_{x} = y \\
z'_{y} = x
\end{cases}
$$
$$
\begin{cases}
y = 0 \\
x = 0
\end{cases}
$$
$$
L(x,y,\lambda) = F(x,y) + \lambda \varphi(x,y)
$$
$$
L(x,y,\lambda) = xy + \lambda(x^2 + y^2 - 1) = 0
$$
$$
\frac{\partial L}{\partial x} = y + 2\lambda x
$$
$$
\frac{\partial L}{\partial y} = x + 2\lambda y
$$
$$
\frac{\partial L}{\partial \lambda} =  x^2 + y^2 - 1
$$
$$
\begin{cases}
 y + 2\lambda x =0 \\
 x + 2\lambda y =0\\
 x^2 + y^2 - 1=0
\end{cases}
$$
$$
x^2 = 1- y^2,\quad x = \pm \sqrt{ 1- y^2 }
$$
$$
y = -2\lambda x 
$$
$$
x + 2\lambda(-2\lambda x) = 0
$$
$$
x - 4\lambda^2x = 0
$$
$$
x(1- 4\lambda^2) = 0
$$
$$
x = 0\quad \lambda^2 = \frac{1}{4}
$$
$$
\lambda = \pm \frac{1}{2}
$$
$$
x = \pm \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 }}\quad y =\pm \frac{1}{\sqrt{ 2 }}
$$
подставляем эти значения в z = xy
$$
x_{1},y_{1} : z = \frac{1}{2}
$$
$$
x_{2},y_{2}: z = \frac{1}{2}
$$
$$
x_{1},y_{2}: z=-\frac{1}{2}
$$
$$
x_{2},y_{1}: z = -\frac{1}{2}
$$

$$
z_{min} = -\frac{1}{2}\quad z_{max} = \frac{1}{2}
$$
